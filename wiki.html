Gameplay

No Click's Sky is a multipayer idling browser RPG

In No Click's Sky, you mine ores, which drop gems. You can later spend these gems to upgrade your weapons, buy bonuses, and craft powerful boosts. Every planet in the game offers a new set of upgrades for your gun, and a new ore which you can mine.

You can also join a Unity, where you can team up with other people to mine difficult gems, or even stars and black holes. This is called the Unity Raid. Completing a raid rewards players with lots gems, and unique loot from different raids.

For some, the goal to completing an idling game is by unlocking all achievements. But, you can also set some goals yourself.

Upgrades

Upgrading your weapon increases your Damage

You get a new set of upgrades whenever you ascend to a different planet. To buy these new upgrades, you will need to mine the new material from the planet you just ascended to.

You keep the damage from your previous set of upgrades, but you will have to go back to a corresponding upgrades planet to buy them.

When you upgrade your weapon to a certain level, you are rewarded with unique bonuses

Bonuses

There are four main groups of bonuses

Common @Lv. 25

Rare @Lv. 100

Epic @Lv. 200

Legendary @Lv 350


Every bonus in the game powers you up with unique bonus. Strenght of the bonus depends on the group of upgrade

Upgrade also has it's damage multiplied every 25 levels. This upgrade is free. At level 500, it's damage gets multiplied by 10.

Runes

You can craft Runes with Dark Matter. Putting a rune in a certain socket permanently boosts your stats

Idling Rune - Reduces Idle Tick rate by 10ms

Clicking Rune - Increases damage by 0.01% for every click

Mythical Runes - Increases stats relevant to both play styles

Keeping your Dark Matter is always a good idea, as each increases your damage by 1%. You can also dump some into buying runes

Buying runes in the early game isn't a good choice. However, these runes are fundamental for the late game, as their bonuses will help you progress further, for a small price

Achievements

There are four main groups of achievements

Common - Easy, but they grant no bonus.

Rare - Medium, increases Damage by 1% for every rare achievement obtained.

Epic - Hard, you are rewarded with a rune for every epic achievement obtained.

Legendary - Extreme, each doubles your damage


Achievements indicate a completed milestone. Unlocking them also displays a cool badge under your profile.

Ascencion

Don't worry. You wont be stuck mining plutonium for the rest of your days.

Upgrading your weapon further would seem impossible, but you get to ascend to a different planet. Ascending to a different planet unlocks new weapon. You keep your stats, just mine a different ore, to upgrade the new weapon

When you pick up on damage you can go back to a previous planet to easily mine the resource there and get important bonuses from that planets upgrades

Do this untill you hit a brick wall. Now it's time to create a miniverse. This means that you go into a universe wihtin a universe

In this next universe, all your stats are reset, and you CAN'T go back to a previous universe. But you gain permanent damage increase based on your highest zone in the previous universe
